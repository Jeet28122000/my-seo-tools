Grade Calculator – Calculate Your Grades Accurately

A grade calculator is an essential tool for students who want to track their academic performance and plan their studies effectively. Whether you are in school, college, or university, calculating your grades manually can be confusing and time-consuming. This online grade calculator simplifies the process by providing instant and accurate results.

This tool allows you to calculate weighted grades, convert letter grades into percentages, and even determine the score you need in your final exam to achieve your desired grade. It is widely used by students across countries like the United States, UK, Canada, and India.

What is a Weighted Grade?

A weighted grade system assigns different importance to assignments, quizzes, exams, and projects. For example, a final exam may carry more weight than a homework assignment. This ensures that more important assessments have a greater impact on your overall grade.

  • Assignments – 20%
  • Midterm Exam – 30%
  • Final Exam – 50%

Using a weighted grade calculator helps you accurately compute your final score without manual errors.

How to Use This Grade Calculator

  1. Enter assignment name (optional)
  2. Enter grade (percentage or letter like A, B, C)
  3. Enter weight (%)
  4. Click calculate

You can add multiple rows to include all your assignments and exams.

Final Exam Grade Calculator
