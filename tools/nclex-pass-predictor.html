About the NCLEX-RN Passing Standard

The National Council Licensure Examination (NCLEX) uses Computerized Adaptive Testing (CAT). While there is no "set" percentage to pass, scoring consistently above 60-70% on high-quality practice banks like UWorld or Archer is generally a strong indicator of readiness.
