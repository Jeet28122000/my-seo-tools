Why Use a Time Calculator?

Calculating time manually is difficult because it uses a **base-60 system** (sexagesimal) rather than the standard base-10 decimal system. This tool prevents errors when calculating **payroll hours**, flight durations, or study schedules.

The Logic

To find the difference, we convert both times into total minutes from midnight, subtract them, and then convert the result back into hours and minutes.
