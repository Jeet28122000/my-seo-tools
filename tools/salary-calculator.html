Gross vs. Net Income

Gross Income is the total amount you earn before any deductions. Net Income, often called "take-home pay," is what remains after taxes, social security, and health insurance are subtracted.

How it is Calculated

  • Monthly: Annual Salary / 12
  • Weekly: Annual Salary / 52
  • Hourly: Weekly Salary / Hours per Week
