Practical Uses for Percentages

Percentages are used daily in various fields. From **calculating tips** at a restaurant and **sales tax** at a store to analyzing **business growth** and **academic grades**, understanding the relationship between numbers and their percentages is a fundamental skill.

The Math

Percentage Value = (Value × Percentage) / 100

Percentage Change = ((New Value - Old Value) / Old Value) × 100
