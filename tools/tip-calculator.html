Why Use a Tip Calculator?

Tipping can be stressful, especially in large groups. This tool ensures that the gratuity is fair and that everyone knows exactly what they owe, including their share of the tip.

The Calculation

Total Tip = Bill × (Tip % / 100)

Total Bill = Bill + Total Tip

Per Person = Total Bill / Number of People
