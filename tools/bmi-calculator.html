What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation that uses your weight and height to estimate body fat. It helps categorize individuals into underweight, normal, overweight, or obese.

BMI Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (m²)

Health Implications

  • Underweight: Nutritional deficiency risk
  • Normal: Healthy body range
  • Overweight: Risk of lifestyle diseases
  • Obese: High risk of heart disease & diabetes

FAQs

What is a healthy BMI?

Between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered healthy.

Is BMI accurate?

BMI is a general indicator and may not be accurate for athletes.
