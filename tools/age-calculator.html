What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a simple tool that helps you determine your exact age based on your date of birth. It calculates your age in years, months, and days with high accuracy.

How Age is Calculated

The calculation is based on the difference between your date of birth and the selected date (or today's date if not specified).

Age = Current Date - Date of Birth

Why Use an Age Calculator?

  • Know your exact age instantly
  • Useful for official documents
  • Track milestones and birthdays
  • Helps in planning retirement and insurance
