Why use a Discount Calculator?

During sales, it can be tricky to do mental math for percentages like 15% or 35% off. This tool allows you to see the actual money you keep in your pocket and the final amount you need to pay at the counter.

Discount Formula

Savings = Original Price × (Discount / 100)

Final Price = Original Price - Savings

Smart Shopping Tips

  • Compare Prices: Sometimes a 50% off price is still higher than another store's base price.
  • Check for Taxes: Remember that discounts are usually applied before sales tax.
  • Stacked Coupons: If you have two discounts, apply the first one, then apply the second to the new balance.
